Goal planning involves identifying financial goals, their timelines, and required investment amounts.
Goals can include retirement, education, home purchase, travel, or emergency corpus creation.
It helps align asset allocation and SIP amounts with long-term objectives.
Clear goal planning prevents underinvestment and financial stress.
It requires estimating inflation, expected returns, and risk tolerance.
Short-term goals need more stable investments like debt funds.
Long-term goals benefit from equity exposure and compounding.
Goal planning supports disciplined investing and periodic progress tracking.
Tools like SIP calculators and Monte Carlo simulations aid accuracy.
Proper goal planning builds a clear roadmap for financial independence.
