Rupee-cost averaging is an investment approach where a fixed amount is invested regularly regardless of market conditions.
It allows investors to buy more units when prices are low and fewer when prices are high.
This helps average out the purchase price over time, reducing the impact of market volatility.
Rupee-cost averaging is a core benefit of SIPs and promotes disciplined investing.
It lowers emotional decision-making associated with timing the market.
Long-term investors can accumulate significant wealth without worrying about market highs or lows.
This strategy works effectively in volatile equity markets where prices fluctuate frequently.
Rupee-cost averaging does not guarantee profits but improves return consistency.
It is particularly beneficial for new investors and those with limited market knowledge.
The strategy aligns well with long-term financial goals and structured planning.
